About Me

I am a maker of websites and games. I code well in Javascript and valiantly in C#.

In 2010 I graduated with a degree in Computer Information Systems from Virginia Commonwealth University. Once out, I decided to give freelance writing a try. It didn't go well, but I learned a lot.

I've done many things since, including a two year stint as a cab driver. Now I'm pivoting back into tech to be a developer.

I am currently seeking employment. I am targeting entry-level back-end development, but welcome the challenges that other segments of the industry pose. If you're interested, please don't hesitate to contact me!
